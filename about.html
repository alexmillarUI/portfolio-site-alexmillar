About Me

My Story

I’ve been studying UI Design for nearly a year now and have learnt a lot on the journey. I started out the process with zero design experience, but I’ve actually always been drawn to design throughout my life and have a good eye for detail. When I was studying Geology at University in the UK, my favourite part of each project was creating the precise geological diagrams used to communicate my findings. Because of this, I think visual design came quite naturally to me.
I have a passion for travelling - I spent a year teaching in Vietnam and I’m moving to Australia for 2 years in September 2024 – I’ll take any opportunity to experience a new culture or start a new adventure!
