My Story

Hi, I’m Alex - I’m a UI Design Graduate from Bristol with a passion for visual design and problem solving and I’ve always been eager to discover and gain new skills. After graduating University with a degree in Geology, I travelled as much as possible and applied myself in various roles ranging from hospitality to teaching in Vietnam.

I've always had an eye for design but I've only relatively recently discovered the world of UI/UX and with it found my real passion. I love bringing designs from concept up to final product - watching how much they evolve and grow and solving all the problems that come with it as it progresses.

I’m now seeking new opportunities where I can combine my skills to help deliver visually appealing designs with UX solutions that always keep the customer’s needs at the forefront.
